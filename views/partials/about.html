These services are extended to the users when they download the Blueprint App via an app store or this website. The consumer then proceeds to use the smartphone app at participating retailers that display the "Blueprint Lives Here" branding.

On the consumer's fifth purchase, they receive a discount determined by the retailer. On the previous four purchases that the consumer makes, the retailer pays a percentage of the receipt that goes into a tax deductible investment fund.

The profits from the fund are then turned into tokens that are loaded on to debit cards, which can be used at participating retailers that display the "Blueprint Lives Here" Branding.

Fergus invented Blueprint back in 2004 after seeing the problems that students were facing with tuition fee increases. The idea grew over the years to different areas within different sectors.

Fergus, developed a wealth distribution theory that could possibly not only change the social and economic landscape, but it could potentially change the social and economic landscapes of every capitalist economy in the world!

​Currently, Blueprint is in the start up stage. It has taken years of careful planning and help from many individuals and organizations to get Blueprint ready to be funded.
